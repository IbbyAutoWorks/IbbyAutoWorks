# Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 13, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Ibby Auto Works™ ("Ibby Auto Works," "we," "us," or "our") handles information when you use our website, services, communications, or related project resources.

Non-lawyer disclaimer: This document is provided as a practical, good-faith privacy summary and is not legal advice. Ibby Auto Works should have qualified counsel review it before relying on it for production, regulated, or jurisdiction-specific compliance needs.

## Information We May Collect

Depending on how you interact with Ibby Auto Works, we may collect:

- Contact information you voluntarily provide, such as name, email address, phone number, or message details.
- Vehicle or service-request information you voluntarily provide so we can respond to an inquiry, estimate, appointment request, or support need.
- Basic technical information, such as browser type, device type, IP address, pages visited, timestamps, and referral URLs, if the website or hosting provider logs this information.
- Communications sent through forms, email, GitHub Issues, or other support channels.

## How We Use Information

We use information to:

- Respond to questions, appointment requests, quotes, service inquiries, and support requests.
- Operate, maintain, secure, and improve our website and services.
- Troubleshoot bugs, investigate abuse, and protect against fraud, spam, or unauthorized access.
- Keep business records when needed for ordinary operations, legal obligations, or dispute resolution.

## No Sale of Personal Information

Ibby Auto Works does not sell personal information. We do not trade personal information for targeted advertising or unrelated third-party marketing.

## Limited Sharing

We may share information only when reasonably necessary, such as with:

- Service providers that help operate hosting, email, scheduling, analytics, security, or customer communication tools.
- Professional advisors, insurers, or authorities when required for legal, safety, security, or compliance reasons.
- GitHub, if you choose to submit public feedback or bug reports through GitHub Issues.

Service providers are expected to use information only for the services they provide to us.

## Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational safeguards to protect information. No website, network, or storage system is perfectly secure, so we cannot guarantee absolute security. Please avoid sending sensitive personal, payment, or vehicle ownership information through public channels such as GitHub Issues.

## Data Retention

We keep information only as long as reasonably needed for the purposes described above, including service follow-up, business records, security, legal obligations, and dispute resolution.

## Your Choices

You may contact us to request access, correction, or deletion of information you provided, subject to reasonable identity verification and any records we must retain for legal, security, or business reasons.

## Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

## Third-Party Links

Our website or project resources may link to third-party websites, including GitHub. Their privacy practices are governed by their own policies.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The updated version will be posted with a new effective date.

## Contact

For privacy questions or requests, contact Ibby Auto Works through the contact method listed on the main website or through the project repository's GitHub Issues page when appropriate.
